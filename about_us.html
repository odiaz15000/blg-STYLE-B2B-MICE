About Baixa Lisbon Group

Baixa Lisbon Group brings together nine family-run restaurants in Lisbon’s historic heart. Located in Baixa, each space offers authentic and traditional Portuguese cuisine. Our group blends history, hospitality, and a distinctive, carefully crafted identity. We create memorable experiences for travelers, locals, and all types of events.

Among our venues, the iconic Café Nicola stands out, originally founded in 1787. Located in Rossio, it was a meeting point for intellectuals, artists, and politicians. Its connection to the poet Bocage remains a key part of its historic identity. Today, it preserves its essence with a unique atmosphere and refined cuisine.

Reopened in 1929 and redesigned in 1935 in Art Deco style, it holds great value. Its architecture, artworks, and cultural legacy make it a Lisbon landmark. Recognized as a “Shop with History,” it is part of the city’s protected heritage. It offers specialties like Bife à Nicola and traditional fado experiences.

Our remaining restaurants are spread across Rua Augusta and nearby streets. Each venue has its own character while maintaining consistent quality and service. We offer versatile spaces tailored to different formats and guest needs. From casual gatherings to exclusive events in distinctive settings.

We host groups from 15 to 350 guests with a personalized service approach. Our offer includes flexible menus, wine tastings, fado shows, and private rooms. We cater to corporate events, travel groups, and special celebrations. We ensure a welcoming, flexible, and authentically Lisbon experience.
