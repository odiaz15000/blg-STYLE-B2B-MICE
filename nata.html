NATA DE LISBOA

A charming pastry shop dedicated to the Pastel de Nata, Lisbon’s signature custard tart. Offers tasting experiences combining the pastry with coffee, Port wine, or cherry liqueur. A delicious stop for smaller groups or walking tours exploring the city.

CAPACITY & SPACES

Takeaway service only: perfect for a quick ‘grab & go’ stop during your walking tour.

No group size limit (groups over 30 will be divided for service).
