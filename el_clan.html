EL CLAN

A vibrant restaurant blending Portuguese and Latin-inspired dishes. Offers menus with options like monkfish rice, chicken curry, and churros. Ideal for casual group meals in a modern atmosphere with a friendly, upbeat vibe.

CAPACITY & SPACES

Dining Hall: ≈70 Pax

GROUP MENUS

Customizable group menus with starter, main course, dessert and drinks included.

Menus

MENU 1 LISBOA

Couvert: Mini toasts, herb cream cheese, seasoned green olives.

MAIN COURSE (choose one):

Monkfish rice with saffron, green and red peppers, white and red onions.

Chicken curry.

Vegetarian Option: vegetable rice (carrots, courgettes, green and red peppers).

Dessert:

Two rings of churros with dulce de leche.

DRINKS: 1 Soft drink, water or lemonade.

Price: €23.00

MENU 2 PORTUGAL

Couvert: Mini toasts, herb cream cheese, seasoned green olives.

Starter: Vegetable soup.

MAIN COURSE (choose one):

Monkfish rice with saffron, green and red peppers, white and red onions.

Chicken curry.

Dessert:

Two rings of churros with dulce de leche.

DRINKS: A glass of house wine or a mug of local beer or 1 soft drink or regular water or sparkling or tonic water or homemade lemonade + One coffee.
