CUCINA DI FAMIGLIA

Located in the heart of Lisbon’s Baixa district, Cucina di Famiglia offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere where Mediterranean flavours meet comfort and conviviality. A versatile restaurant ideal for groups, casual meals, or relaxed dining experiences, perfect for who want to feel at home while discovering the taste of Lisbon.
