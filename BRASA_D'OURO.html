BRASA D'OURO

A seafood and Portuguese cuisine landmark in Lisbon. Located on the city’s most famous pedestrian street, it serves fresh fish, codfish dishes, and classic desserts in a spacious and elegant setting, perfect for large group meals in the heart of the Baixa.
