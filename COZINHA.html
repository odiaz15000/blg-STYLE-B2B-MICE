COZINHA D’AVÓ CELESTE

A cozy restaurant celebrating Portuguese comfort food and family recipes. Known for its codfish specialties and the famous “Avó Celeste Soup”, it offers authentic menus inspired by homemade cuisine. Great for groups seeking a traditional and relaxed experience.

CAPACITY & SPACES
