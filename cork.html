CORK KITCHEN

An intimate restaurant in downtown Lisbon where Portuguese flavors meet sophistication. Designed for small groups, Cork Kitchen blends authentic recipes with modern presentation, always paired with great wine and warm hospitality.

CAPACITY & SPACES

Dining Hall: ≈20 Pax

GROUP MENUS

Customizable group menus with starter, main course, dessert and drinks included.

Menus

MENU 1

Couvert: Bread, butter and olives.

Starter: Fresh salad to share (1 per 4 guests).

MAIN COURSE (choose one):

Seabass fillet on a bed of spinach and sautéed potatoes.

Sliced pork “secretos” with garlic and parsley sauce, served with mashed potatoes.

Dessert:

Pudding.

DRINKS: Water or soft drink or glass of red or white wine (1 per person).

Price: €27

MENU 2

Couvert: Bread, olive butter, shrimp croquette “à Guilho”.

Starter: Chestnut soup.

MAIN COURSE (choose one):

“Bacalhau à Lagareiro” (traditional roasted cod with olive oil and potatoes).

“Carolino da Terra” – Stewed carolino rice with black pork ribs, chouriço and red wine.

Dessert:

Chocolate Mousse.

DRINKS: Water or soft drink or glass of red or white wine (1 per person).

Price: €33

Conditions

Valid for groups with minimum 15 pax.

Series price for 20 groups/year.

Same menu for whole group (except dietary restrictions).

Extras available at additional cost.

All prices include VAT.
