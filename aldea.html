Aldea Restaurant

A warm, central space with contemporary and rustic décor, ideal for medium and large events. Modern Portuguese dishes and tapas, with a dedicated Fado showroom.

Upper floor accommodates up to 100 guests, group menus available from €16.50.

Dining Hall: ≈100 Pax
